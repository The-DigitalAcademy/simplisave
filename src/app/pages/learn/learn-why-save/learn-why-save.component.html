Why is important for me to save?

Financial challenges

It creates a safety net for post-graduation, safeguarding against unforeseen financial challenges when the allowance stops, covering unexpected expenses like medical bills or job search costs.

Good financial habits

It creates good financial habits, benefiting students in the long run by instilling responsible money management skills.

Transition period

Savings acts as a financial buffer during the transition from university to the workforce, helping cover living expenses until a stable income is obtained.
