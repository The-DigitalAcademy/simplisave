Account best suited for students

The table to the right shows the total amount a student saving R300 a month for three years can expect to have and compares it to someone saving the same amount for the same period but not in a savings account

For a student, the Tax-Free Savings Account is the most suitable option as it allows tax-free growth on savings and immediate access to money. The Tax-Free ETF Savings Account could also be a good choice if the student is interested in investing in exchange-traded funds within a tax-free framework. It is important for the student to consider their financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment preferences when selecting the most appropriate account.
