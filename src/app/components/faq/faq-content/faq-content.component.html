What is SimpliSave?

SimpliSave is a mobile application designed specifically for students who use bursaries. It provides them with a savings account where they can save money or manage their monthly allowances effectively.

Is SimpliSave free to use?

Currently, SimpliSave is free to use. However, please note that we reserve the right to introduce fees or charges in the future. If any such fees or charges are introduced, you will be notified in advance.

How secure is my account?

We take the security and privacy of your account information seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Can I access SimpliSave on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your SimpliSave account on multiple devices by logging in with your account credentials. However, please ensure that you maintain the security of your account information and follow best practices for using the app on different devices.

Can I earn interest on SimpliSave?

SimpliSave primarily focuses on providing a convenient platform for managing your funds. While we do not guarantee the availability of interest or any returns on your savings, you can track your savings and expenses effectively using the app.
